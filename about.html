About us

Empowering Career Enriching Lives Together

Build your ideal workforce with confidence as PeopleNTech, a Woman & Minority-Owned Business since 2005, takes the lead. Headquartered in Alexandria, Virginia, we empower your business with Contingent Staffing, Direct Hire, SOW, and Payroll Services [AOR/EOR], delivering over 575 hires in FY23-24 with a 24-48 hour turnaround. With a hyper-local presence across 10+ locations throughout North America, our AI-driven solutions connect you to top-tier talent, serving IT, BFSI, Auto, FMCG, Retail, Manufacturing, Pharma, and more, ensuring cost efficiency and agility.
