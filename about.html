About the Company

Empowering Futures with Human Connection

At PeopleNTech LLC, our “why” is rooted in bridging talent with opportunity, envisioning a future where every business thrives through the power of its people. Since our founding in 2005 as a Woman & Minority-Owned Business, we have embraced a bold mission: to redefine workforce solutions with unwavering commitment and transformative vision.

We are here to empower your journey. By meeting clients where they are, we craft tailored strategies that harness the strength of diversity and innovation. Through our Workforce Solutions—Contingent Staffing, Direct Hire, SOW, and Payroll Services—and AI-driven Recruitment Processes, and cutting-edge Digital & IT Solutions, we tackle your toughest challenges, turning aspirations into impactful realities.
